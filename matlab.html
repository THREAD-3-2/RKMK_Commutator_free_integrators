Example on the application of Lie group integrators for the solution of the equations of motion of a system defined on R3 x (SO3)^2 x S2^2 August 2022, Andrea Leone @ NTNU

Ref.: E. Celledoni, E. Çokaj, A. Leone, D. Murari, B. Owren. “Lie group integrators for mechanical systems”, International Journal of Computer Mathematics, 99:1, 58-88.

src.integrators.LieEuler¶

Lie-Euler time integrator (Runge-Kutta-Munthe-Kaas method of order 1)

Parameters:

  • vector_field – right hand side of the ODE

  • action – Lie group action

  • expinential – exponential map from the Lie algebra to the Lie group

  • p – solution at time t_n

  • h – time step size

  • sigma0 – initial value of the curve sigma on the Lie algebra

  • trajectory – desired trajectory

  • t – discrete time t_n

Returns:

solution at time t_(n+1))order 1
